Maia is a licensed social worker and dedicated to supporting the emotional health and wellness of teens. She works with teenagers struggling with anxiety, depression, abuse, family conflict, and trauma. Maia is particularly focused on teaching young people how to improve their communication and coping skills. She is dedicated to continuing the important conversations surrounding mental health and well-being to reduce the stigma of mental illness and increase access to treatment.
